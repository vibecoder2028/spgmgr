SPG Management System

Complete Business Solution for Streamlining Sales Promotion Girls Management Across Indonesia

Executive Summary

The SPG Management System is a comprehensive digital platform designed to streamline the management of Sales Promotion Girls across Indonesia. This system addresses the complexities of managing hundreds of promotional staff with varying schedules, experience levels, and geographic locations.

Core System Modules

1. SPG Profile Management

Individual SPG Profiles Include:

  • Personal Information (Name, ID, Contact, Address)
  • Professional Details (Experience level, Product knowledge certifications)
  • Physical Specifications (Uniform sizes, footwear sizes)
  • Availability Calendar (Regular schedule, blackout dates)
  • Performance Ratings and Reviews
  • Photo gallery (professional headshots, full-body shots for uniform fitting)
  • Language skills and regional dialects
  • Transportation access (own vehicle, public transport dependency)

2. Advanced Scheduling System

Smart Scheduling Features:

  • Drag-and-Drop Calendar Interface: Visual scheduling with color-coded availability
  • Auto-Matching Algorithm: Automatically suggests best-fit SPGs based on:
    • Location proximity
    • Product knowledge
    • Experience level
    • Availability
    • Past performance ratings
  • Shift Templates: Pre-configured shifts for different event types
  • Conflict Detection: Prevents double-booking and scheduling conflicts
  • Bulk Scheduling: Assign multiple SPGs to large events simultaneously
  • Emergency Replacement: Quick replacement suggestions for last-minute cancellations

3. Dynamic Paygrade System

Flexible Compensation Structure:

  • Base Rate Categories: Entry, Intermediate, Senior, Expert levels
  • Time-Based Multipliers:
    • Regular hours (1.0x)
    • Overtime (1.5x)
    • Weekend premium (1.3x)
    • Holiday premium (2.0x)
    • Night shift differential (1.2x)
  • Performance Bonuses: Merit-based incentives
  • Location Premiums: Additional pay for remote or high-traffic areas
  • Product Complexity Bonus: Extra compensation for technical products
  • Automated Payroll Calculation: Real-time earnings tracking

4. Location Intelligence System

Geographic Management Tools:

  • Real-Time GPS Tracking: Live location monitoring during work hours
  • Geofenced Check-in/out: Automatic attendance when entering/leaving venues
  • Route Optimization: Suggest optimal SPG assignments based on location clusters
  • Regional Mapping: Visual representation of SPG distribution across Indonesia
  • Transportation Cost Calculator: Factor travel expenses into assignments
  • Venue Database: Detailed information about recurring promotional locations

5. Inventory & Uniform Management

Comprehensive Asset Tracking:

  • Digital Uniform Catalog: Complete inventory with sizes and styles
  • Automated Size Matching: Match SPG measurements to available inventory
  • Distribution Tracking: Monitor uniform check-out/return
  • Cleaning Schedule: Track uniform maintenance cycles
  • Replacement Alerts: Notify when uniforms need replacement
  • Bulk Order Management: Streamline uniform procurement
  • Promotional Material Tracking: Manage brochures, samples, and displays

Advanced Features

6. Performance Analytics Dashboard

Data-Driven Insights:

  • Individual Performance Metrics: Sales conversion, customer engagement, punctuality
  • Team Performance Comparisons: Identify top performers and improvement areas
  • Event Success Analysis: Measure promotional campaign effectiveness
  • Cost-Per-Acquisition Tracking: Calculate ROI for different SPG assignments
  • Predictive Analytics: Forecast staffing needs based on historical data

7. Communication Hub

Integrated Communication Tools:

  • Broadcast Messaging: Send updates to all or filtered groups of SPGs
  • Event Briefing System: Distribute product knowledge and event instructions
  • Photo/Video Reports: SPGs submit visual reports from events
  • Feedback Collection: Gather customer and venue feedback
  • Emergency Contact System: Quick communication for urgent situations

8. Training & Certification Module

Skill Development Platform:

  • Product Knowledge Base: Searchable database of product information
  • Training Modules: Interactive learning modules for different products/brands
  • Certification Tracking: Monitor completion of required training
  • Skill Assessment Tests: Evaluate product knowledge and sales techniques
  • Continuous Learning Paths: Career development opportunities
